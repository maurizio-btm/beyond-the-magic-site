Meet BizHealth.ai

Your trusted business health analyst

BizHealth.ai is a proprietary, AI-powered diagnostic platform built for small and mid-size businesses. It examines your organization across twelve critical business areas — surfacing the hidden gaps in leadership, culture, operations, and customer experience that quietly limit growth — and turns what it finds into actionable insights and strategies you can put to work immediately.

No subscriptions. Transparent pricing. Free tools included. And a personal client portal that makes every finding easy to access, revisit, and share with your team.

BizHealth.ai was founded by BTM co-founder Dennis Hough — which is why we trust it as the diagnostic starting point for organizations that want clarity before committing to a larger engagement.
