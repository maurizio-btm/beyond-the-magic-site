The Reality

Guests do not separate disruption from service.

They experience one journey. In moments of delay, uncertainty, or change, every interaction becomes a measure of trust in the Etihad brand.

What leaders feel
Operational pressure is visible. Consistency across touchpoints becomes harder when recovery moments accelerate.
What guests feel
Confidence can shift quickly. Communication, empathy, and presence shape whether disruption feels managed or magnified.
What matters most
Luxury must remain recognizable under pressure. The standard cannot disappear when the operation is most tested.
The Opportunity

Disruption can become a signature moment of leadership.

With the right operating model, leaders and frontline teams can recover service with clarity, calm, and confidence, protecting both guest trust and team alignment.

Recovery is where a premium brand either proves its promise, or quietly weakens it. The work is not only to solve the visible problem. It is to equip people to restore confidence in the moment.

The Beyond the Magic Approach

Build the operating model, then equip the culture to carry it.

Our proposal is designed to align senior leaders, define the service recovery standard, create the supporting tools, and prepare the organization for disciplined rollout and sustainment.

Phase 1
Assess what guests and teams actually experience. Establish a factual baseline through immersion, observation, and leadership alignment.
Phase 2
Design the recovery model around Etihad's standard. Translate ambition into tools, routines, and practical moments of action.
Phase 3
Roll out with confidence and reinforce over time. Embed the work through leader activation, training, and sustainment support.
