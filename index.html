Our Story

Built from the inside. Committed to what lasts.

Maurizio Landi built his practice on a belief that the strongest performance principles in the world should not be confined to a single organization.

Shaped by decades inside the Walt Disney Company — including years of work connected to the Disney Institute — he carries a practitioner's understanding of how culture, leadership, learning, and service come together not in theory, but in the daily systems and operational disciplines that make excellence repeatable at scale. When that chapter closed, he saw both a responsibility and an opportunity: to carry that methodology forward with greater flexibility, and to make that caliber of thinking available to organizations ready to build something enduring.

A trusted counselor by nature, Maurizio's approach has always been grounded in integrity, a genuine investment in the people he serves, and a conviction that what you build for others should outlast the engagement. That ethic extends beyond client work — into board service, community leadership, and relationships that have endured across two decades of practice.

Our Approach

From aspiration to operation

A name tag is a small object with heavy meaning. It identifies the individual, signals belonging to something bigger, and carries expectations about how the brand should be experienced. Every organization has its own version of that symbolic carrier of identity. Our framework is built around how to design it, support it, and make it last.

01

Design

Define the identity, standards, symbols, and experiences the organization wants to be known for. Map the gap between what you say you value and what people actually experience.

02

Build

Create the systems, tools, leadership practices, and behaviors that make the identity real — so culture is supported by structure, not just intention.

03

Activate

Introduce people to the meaning behind the work so they do not just wear the brand — they understand what it asks of them and are equipped to deliver it.

04

Sustain

Reinforce, measure, coach, and maintain the culture so the identity does not fade as the organization grows, changes, or faces pressure.

Why Us

Three distinctions that define our work

Our engagements are collaborative, immersive, and designed for transfer of ownership. These three principles shape how we partner and what clients can expect.

01

Operating Systems, Not Training Alone

Training creates awareness. Operating systems create consistency. We design the latter — building on your existing strengths rather than replacing them, so the desired experience does not depend on individual heroics.

02

Arrive, Build, Transfer, Endure

We do not arrive, inspire, and depart. We build with your team, transfer capability, and equip your people to own and evolve the system independently. Our goal is never dependency — it is capability transfer.

03

Scale Without Losing Fidelity

Whether an engagement touches 50 leaders or 30,000 participants, we protect clarity, consistency, and trust across every layer — reinforced through governance, feedback loops, and leader reinforcement that prevents drift.
