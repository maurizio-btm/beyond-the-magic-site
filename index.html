Our Origin

A lineage built from the inside

Beyond the Magic Consulting exists because the strongest service and leadership principles in the world should not be confined to a single park, a single campus, or a single organization.

Built by leaders who spent decades inside the Walt Disney Company, including years shaping work connected to the Disney Institute, the firm carries forward a lived understanding of how culture, leadership, service, and learning come together in practice, not just in theory.

When that chapter changed, we saw both an opportunity and a responsibility, to carry the torch forward, to adapt it with greater flexibility, and to make that caliber of thinking available to organizations ready to build something enduring.

What clients receive is not borrowed language. It is firsthand experience translated into modern operating systems for performance.

We do not just deliver training. We build operating systems for performance.

How We Work

From aspiration to operation

Our methodology moves organizations from ambition to disciplined execution by combining diagnostic clarity, intentional design, field activation, and sustainment structures that protect performance over time.

Methodology

The Name Tag Framework

Four disciplined phases guide the work, each one designed to translate ideals into observable behaviors, reliable execution, and measurable outcomes.

01

Architect It

Map the current state, identify the gap between intended and actual performance, and understand what must change before design begins.

02

Build It

Design the system, define expectations, clarify decision rights, and create the architecture required for consistent excellence.

03

Activate It

Implement, test, measure, and iterate so execution becomes embedded in real time through leader reinforcement and feedback loops.

04

Sustain It

Observe, adjust, and protect what was built so ownership lives inside your team and performance continues after the engagement ends.
