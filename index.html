Our Origin

A Lineage Built from the Inside

Beyond the Magic Consulting exists because the greatest service and leadership principles in the world should not be confined to a single park, a single campus, or a single organization. We were founded by professionals who spent decades inside the Walt Disney Company—including years with the Disney Institute—designing, delivering, and living the systems that made Disney the global benchmark for guest experience, organizational culture, and leadership excellence.

When the Disney Institute stepped back from offering these services to external clients, we saw an opportunity and a responsibility: to carry that torch forward, and to go further.

Our founder and core team members built their careers inside the Disney ecosystem—not just studying it, but executing it at scale. That firsthand experience lives in the DNA of every engagement we deliver.

We offer organizations the same caliber of expertise—but with greater depth, greater customization, and a more enduring partnership model than was ever possible before.
Our Philosophy

Practitioners, Not Theorists

Headquartered in Central Florida—the cradle of experiential excellence—Beyond the Magic Consulting is a full-service consulting, learning & development, and organizational transformation firm. Our team is not a collection of theorists or classroom instructors. We are practitioners.

Performance is not inspired. It is designed.

Most organizations invest heavily in training and culture initiatives while the underlying system architecture remains unchanged. We address it directly. We are a performance architecture firm—we design the systems that make consistent performance predictable, measurable, and sustainable.

The Name Tag Framework

From Aspiration to Operation

Our methodology is structured around four disciplined phases that move organizations from aspiration to operation.

01

Architect It

Map the current state. Find the gap between intended and actual performance. Understand what exists before designing what's next.

02

Build It

Design the system. Define expectations, accountability, and decision rights. Create the architecture for consistent excellence.

03

Activate It

Implement, measure, and iterate. Build consistency into the system through disciplined execution and real-time feedback.

04

Sustain It

Observe, adjust, and protect performance in real time. Ensure what is built is owned, reinforced, and improved by your people.
